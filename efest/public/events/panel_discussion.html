Ideathon 2024

Description

This event invites students from various institutes to present their innovative ideas, including necessary prototypes. Participants will address different problem statements provided in advance. The competition will progress through multiple rounds, with teams being eliminated until the final round, which will take place during E-Fest 6.0.

Details

Date: 12th October 2024

Location: Main Auditorium, City Park

Rules and Regulations

  • No outside food or drinks allowed.
  • Please respect the performers and fellow attendees.
  • Maintain cleanliness and dispose of trash properly.
  • Follow the event schedule to enjoy all performances.
